About Me

Hello! I’m Haochen (Eric) Zhou, an Economics graduate from Kalamazoo College with a minor in History. My education blends analytical reasoning with a liberal arts foundation that emphasizes critical thinking, presentation skills, and collaborative problem-solving.

I currently work as a Supply Chain Specialist at Trinity Health, where I manage inventory systems across several hospital departments, coordinate with medical staff to ensure supply availability, and support process improvements that enhance efficiency and organization. This role has given me valuable experience in structured operations and the importance of accurate, timely decision-making in complex environments.

Through my academic background and professional work, I’ve developed a strong conceptual understanding of data analytics, economic research, and workflow optimization — helping me approach challenges from both a systems and efficiency perspective.
